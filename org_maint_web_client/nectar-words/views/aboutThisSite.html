A collection of sayings collected by our modern day Hemadpant related to spiritiual freedom. Jayasimha Prasad started Sai Amruta Vani (literal translation 'Words or Voice of Nectar') on August 10 2010.

Jayasimha gleans and searches different sources to find prophetic and wise sayings, narrations of stories and other snippets relevant to spirituality and love. He has developed a super knack of posting something which is so relevant at that point of time!!! Appears that he is being guided by a Supreme force...

This is divided by week and starts with last weeks' sayings. At this point, the list starts with the past week (as mentioned) then extends to a few weeks back (how many is, right now, dynamic!!) *.

Hoping to iteratively add more content with a long term goal to be able to post all past and present sayings

Hope you enjoy and absorb these pearls and gain from them!!

* Sometimes it may not align to a calendar week, or the order may be changed, to preserve continuity of a series of postings
