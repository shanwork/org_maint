A poem I wrote

A collection of sayings collected by our modern day Hemadpant related to spiritiual freedom. Jayasimha Prasad started Sai Amruta Vani (literal translation 'Words or Voice of Nectar') on August 10 2010.

Jayasimha gleans and searches different sources to find prophetic and wise sayings, narrations of stories and other snippets relevant to spirituality and love. He has developed a super knack of posting something which is so relevant at that point of time!!! Appears that he is being guided by a Supreme force...

This is divided by week and starts with last weeks' sayings. At this point, the list starts with the past week (as mentioned) then extends to a few weeks back (how many is, right now, dynamic!!) *.

Hoping to iteratively add more content with a long term goal to be able to post all past and present sayings

Hope you enjoy and absorb these pearls and gain from them!!

To Shirdi Sai: a prayer

Aatey hai duur duur se
Shirdi yaatra bhakti man mein
Dekhne aapki murthy,
De man ko shanti
Bhakto ka pranaaam sweekarna,
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana

Baithe ho samadhi sthan
Kintu nazar sabko chhan
America ya ho Hindustaan
Duniya mei chhayi aapki shaan
Mann Se, Dil se Pukaarey, Aap aana
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana

Lagtaa Dekte ho Na Dekhhe Bhi
Natkhhat aanken maasoomyat bhi
Muskurahat se Suraj bhi Sharmaye
Zindagi Ki Amawas Ko Poonam Banaye
Aapky Bachho ka palan Karna
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana

Rogi ho ya dukhi ho sabh aatey
Gun aapke Gaatey
Udi se aseemit chamatkaar
Ilaaj ke saath dher saara pyaar
Bigadi ko Durusth Karaana,
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana

Seva Mein Mewa, Sai Ka Paigam
Na Phal ki Apeksha Karo Achhe Kaam
Na Nafrat, Na Krodh, Na Bhay, Man Ko Shanth Rakhna
Sahi Dishaa main chalna, Na Burey mein Bhataknaa
Bhaktoe se Achhey Karya Karwaana,
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana

Naa Bair, Naa Ghamand, Dosti Badhaana,
Naa oonch Naa Neech, Ek Bartaw karaana
Ghamand, Ahankar, Basma Karoe
Ek Saath, Ek Praan Jiyo Phalo
Tootey Rishto Ko Judaana
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana

Pyaar se Duniya chalti hai
Pyaar Bina Duniya Jalti Hai
Pyaar ka Prasaad De De Sai
Pyaar Poori Duniya Pe Chhai
Humesha, Dil Mein Jaga Banana,
Kehta Ye Sai Ka Deewana
