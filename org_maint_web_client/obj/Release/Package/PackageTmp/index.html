  • About the Budgeting Wesbite
    • Budgeting Wesbite
     As of I have developed the budgeting website. Some background:
    A humanitarian effort like NAAM movement for Farmer Suicides in Maharashtra (India) has different ways by which we can help. Sometimes organized accounting may take a backseat to the more urgent needs and emotions to resolve things, help etc. Thus entered an idea into my head to use a subject matter to apply whatever I am in the process of learning.
    At this point, this is a simple accounting web site with the aim of tracking finances, how much the inflow is, what is the deficit, how the funds have been used, etc.
    While, as said, it was initiated with NAAM as an inspiration, it can be applied to any calamity or disaster situation, or any regular situation where a budget is used to maintain a list of items, say a transport service which maintains a bunch of trucks.
